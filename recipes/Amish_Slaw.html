Amish Slaw

I live in a wonderful Amish community and the ladies here are wonderful cooks. This is a lovely cole slaw recipe for those who like slaw without mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head cabbage, cored and shredded
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  1. In a large bowl
  2. toss together the cabbage
  3. In a small saucepan, combine the vinegar
  4. Bring to a boil
  5. and toss to coat
